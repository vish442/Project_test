This folder contains functions of the form
RefractiveIndicesNx1 = RefractiveIndxFcn(aRaysetO)

Inputs: aRaysetO
Outputs: RefractiveIndicesNx1


aRaysetO is a RaysetO object.
RefractiveIndicesNx1 is an Nx1 array of refractive indices that the N rays in aRaysetO would encounter upon entering the space that invokes the RefractiveIndxFcn
